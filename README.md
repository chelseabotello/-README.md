My team and I anticipate making the NLU app easier for students to navigate. We plan to limit the number of bugs in the app and incorporate artificial intelligence to provide students with an accessible resource at all times to answer questions and save time finding specific items they may be looking for. We also plan to enable autosave in the app so our students don’t lose the hard work they put in.

We will make those changes by having different groups work on each specific issue. One group will specifically work on recoding the NLU app while also organizing it to make it easier to read. By incorporating cookies, we will reduce the need to save work as much, since it will now autosave. At the bottom of the screen, we will code a taskbar that suggests AI assistance when selected.
